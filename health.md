Welcome to Habitstreak. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, retain, and protect your information when you use the Habitstreak mobile application. By using the app, you agree to the collection and use of information in relation to this policy.

1. Information We Collect
To provide the core features of Habitstreak, including task tracking and the competitive leaderboard, we collect the following types of data:

Profile Information: When you create an account, we may collect a username, display name, or email address to identify you on the leaderboard.

Activity Data: We collect data regarding the tasks you complete, your current streaks, and your accumulated scores to update your standing on the leaderboard.

Device Information: We may collect basic device information, such as device type and operating system version, to ensure the app runs smoothly and to troubleshoot bugs.

2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected data strictly to operate and improve Habitstreak:

To track your daily habits and streak progress.

To calculate and display user rankings on the global/friends leaderboard.

To manage your account and provide customer support.

3. Data Retention Policy
We are committed to minimizing the data we keep. We retain your personal data only for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the Habitstreak services:

Active Accounts: Your profile data, task history, and leaderboard rankings are retained as long as you maintain an active account with Habitstreak.

Inactive Accounts: If an account remains completely inactive for a period of 12months, we will automatically anonymize or delete the associated personal data and leaderboard history.

App Uninstalls: Simply uninstalling the app does not automatically delete your data. You must explicitly request account deletion to remove your data immediately (see Section 4).

4. Data Deletion Policy and Mechanism
You have the right to request the complete deletion of your personal data at any time. When a deletion request is processed, your account, username, task history, and leaderboard rankings are permanently removed from our active databases within [Choose one: 7 / 14 / 30] days.

How to delete your data:

In-App Deletion: Open the Habitstreak app, navigate to [Settings > Account > Delete Account], and confirm your request. Your data will be flagged for immediate deletion.

Email Request: If you no longer have access to the app, you can request data deletion by emailing us at Rupeshranjanpanda@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request." Please include your Habitstreak username so we can locate and remove your data.
