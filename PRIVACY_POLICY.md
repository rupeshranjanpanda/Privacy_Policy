Privacy Policy for Resume Analyzer App

Last updated: June 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Resume Analyzer ("we", "our", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal information you provide when you use our resume analysis service (the "Service"). The Service allows you to upload resumes, receive automated analysis and ratings, and obtain suggestions to improve your resume.

2. Data Controller

The data controller for the Service is the operator of this application. If you need to contact us about privacy matters, see the Contact section below.

3. Information We Collect

- Information you provide directly: the resumes and documents you upload, text you paste into the app, profile information (name, email), and optional feedback you submit.
- Usage information: actions you take in the app, timestamps, and feature usage needed to provide and improve the Service.
- Device and technical data: IP address, browser or app version, device identifiers, and basic diagnostic logs.
- Cookies and similar technologies: to enable sessions and remember preferences.

Important: Do not submit sensitive personal data (e.g., health, financial account numbers, government identifiers) unless explicitly required. If you include sensitive data in a resume, it will be processed as described below.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide the Service: analyze uploaded resumes, produce scores, and deliver personalized suggestions.
- Improve the Service: train and refine algorithms, fix bugs, and optimize performance using aggregated or pseudonymized data.
- Communicate with you: respond to support inquiries, send service-related notices, and provide updates.
- Security and compliance: detect abuse, enforce terms, and comply with legal obligations.

Automated decision-making and profiling: The Service uses automated processing (including machine learning models and scoring algorithms) to analyze resumes and generate ratings and suggestions. These automated outputs are intended to assist users and are not a substitute for human judgment. You may request an explanation of how a particular score or recommendation was produced and may request human review or correction where applicable.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

If you are in a jurisdiction that requires a legal basis, we rely primarily on: (a) your consent when you upload a resume or otherwise agree to processing; and (b) our legitimate interests in operating and improving the Service, provided such interests do not override your rights.

6. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with:
- Service providers: third-party vendors who help host, maintain, or analyze data (e.g., cloud providers, analytics services). These providers act under contract and are required to protect data.
- Legal requests: when required by law, subpoena, or to protect rights, safety, or property.
- Aggregated or anonymized data: we may publish or share aggregated insights that cannot reasonably be used to identify you.

If a business transaction occurs (sale, merger, reorganization), personal data may be transferred as part of that transaction under appropriate protections.

7. Data Retention

We retain uploaded resumes, analysis results, and related account data for as long as necessary to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. You can request deletion of your account and data as described in the Your Rights section below. After deletion requests, we will remove personal data from active systems and purge backups within a reasonable time frame.

8. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data from unauthorized access, use, alteration, and disclosure. However, no system is completely secure. If a data incident occurs, we will follow applicable breach-notification laws and inform affected users as required.

9. International Transfers

Your data may be processed or stored in countries other than your own. We will take steps required by applicable law to provide adequate protection for international transfers (e.g., standard contractual clauses, relying on countries with adequate protections).

10. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:
- Access the personal data we hold about you.
- Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.

To exercise these rights, contact us via the Contact section. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling requests.
